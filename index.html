What is the World Cup?

It is a Soccer tournement with each *qualifing nation competing for first place in a round robin group stage, followed by single elimination games until 1 team is crowned victor.

The tournement is held once every 4 years. To qualifiy for the tournement you must earn enough points in your region (wins 3 points, ties 1 point and 0 for loses)
